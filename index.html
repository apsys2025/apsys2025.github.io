Welcome to the APSys 2025 Website!

Building on the success of its predecessors, the 16th ACM SIGOPS Asia-Pacific Workshop on Systems (APSys 2025) will continue to serve as a vibrant forum for researchers and practitioners to present innovative and emerging ideas in systems research, fostering discussion and collaboration within the Asia-Pacific region and beyond. This year, APSys 2025 will be held in Seoul, South Korea, on October 12-13, co-located with SOSP 2025, creating a unique opportunity for deeper engagement and synergy among participants. We take a broad view of computer systems and welcome papers on topics such as:

  • Operating systems
  • Virtualization
  • File and storage systems
  • Networked systems
  • Mobile, embedded, and IoT systems
  • Cloud computing and data centers
  • Edge computing
  • Big data systems
  • Distributed systems
  • Green and sustainable computing
  • Debugging, testing, and verification
  • Measurement, monitoring, and modeling
  • Reliability, scalability, and fault tolerance
  • Security and privacy
  • Systems for machine learning, machine learning for systems
  • Hardware and software interaction
  • Experience with deployed systems
  • Blockchain and cryptocurrency systems
