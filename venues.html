Transportation

From Incheon International Airport

Airport bus 6705 runs directly from Incheon International Airport Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 to Lotte Hotel World, with no stops along the way. The bus interval is 20 ~ 30 minutes, detailed operation hours and fare is provided here.



From Seoul Station

You can take Seoul Metropolitan Subway Line 4 to Dongdaemun History & Culture Park station, then take Line 2 to get to Jamsil station which Lotte Hotel World is 200 meters away. You can check the detailed operation times and routes here.


If the train and subway are too confusing, visitors can take a taxi directly to the destination. From Incheon Airport, it takes about 1 and 1/2 hour and costs about 80000 KRW (~60 USD); From Seoul Station, it takes about 25 minutes and costs about 25000 KRW (~20 USD).
